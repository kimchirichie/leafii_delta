Our Story

As passionate students, we like to stand out among our peers. So we created our own personal websites to showcase our projects, skills, passion and hobbies. However, employers can't find our amazing websites if they don't know our urls.

You can always Google our names, but the first page of Google is clouded with other popular websites such as LinkedIn, Twitter & Facebook; our websites are pushed down at the very bottom of Google. As a result, we created Leafii, a platform to specifically search for online portfolios.

Leafii is a platform that makes online portfolios searchable. Simply submit your website and we will make all the content on your website searchable. No need to fill out templates. We do all the work for you.

When a website is submitted on our platform, our platform will parse all the keywords on your website. This way, not only people can find you base on your url but also base on the content on your website.
